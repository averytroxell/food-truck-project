Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles are a traditional Mexican dish made from tortilla chips that are lightly fried and then simmered in a flavorful sauce, with tomatoes, chili peppers, and spices. Served with fried or scrambled eggs, cheese, beans, and avocado.

$8

Machaca

A dish that originated in northern Mexico and is made from dried and shredded beef or pork that is rehydrated and then cooked with onions, tomatoes, and chili peppers. Served with rice.

$10

Tacos

A popular Mexican street food made from soft or hard shells filled with seasoned meat, beans, cheese, lettuce, and salsa. Can be served in many different styles, including asada (grilled meat), al pastor (spit-roasted pork), and barbacoa (slow-cooked beef).

$7 (two per order)

Burrito

A dish consisting of a large flour tortilla filled with beans, rice, cheese, meat, and a variety of vegetables. Served with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole.

$9

Menudo

A traditional Mexican soup made from tripe (cow stomach) that is slow-cooked with chili peppers, onions, and other seasonings. Served with lime wedges, chopped onions, and cilantro.

$12

Cochinita Pibil

A traditional dish from the Yucatan region made with marinated and slow-roasted pork that is served with rice, corn tortillas, pickled onions, and salsa. The pork is marinated in a mixture of spices and sour orange juice before being wrapped in banana leaves and cooked.

$11

Tamales

A dish made of masa (a type of corn dough) filled with meat, cheese, and a variety of vegetables, and then wrapped in a corn husk and steamed. Served with salsa.

$6 (two per order)
